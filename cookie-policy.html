Cookie Policy

Welcome to our Cookie Policy page. Below, we explain how we collect, use, and disclose information related to the use of cookies when you engage with our services aimed at automating business processes using AI. We prioritize your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. Please read this policy to understand how we use cookies in our AI-driven business automation services.

I. Collection and Use of Information

1.1 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and other tracking technologies to enhance your experience and provide a personalized service as you explore how AI can optimize your business processes. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us identify your preferences and activity on our website.

1.1.1 Types of Cookies We Use

Our website uses the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the proper functioning of our AI automation services.
  • Performance Cookies: These help us understand how our website is being used and improve the AI-driven features we offer.
  • Functional Cookies: These cookies allow us to personalize content and remember your preferences while interacting with our services.
  • Analytics Cookies: These help us analyze usage data, such as how you interact with our AI-powered business tools, to enhance the effectiveness of our services.

1.1.2 How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Improve the functionality and performance of our website and AI tools.
  • Customize the content you see based on your previous interactions.
  • Analyze website traffic and user behavior to continually refine our services.

II. Managing Cookies

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time. Most web browsers allow you to block or delete cookies; however, doing so may affect your ability to fully utilize our services.

2.1 How to Control Cookies

You can set your browser to reject cookies, or to notify you when a cookie is sent. Please note that rejecting or deleting cookies may limit the functionality of our website and the AI tools we provide for business automation.

III. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services to improve our business automation services, including tracking services that collect data to help us optimize our AI tools. These third-party services may use cookies to collect information about your interactions with our services. They are prohibited from using this data for any other purpose.

IV. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We reserve the right to update this cookie policy at any time to reflect changes in our use of cookies. When we do, we will post the updated policy on this page. We recommend reviewing this policy regularly to stay informed about our use of cookies in relation to AI-driven business automation.
